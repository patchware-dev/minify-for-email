A fixed term investment solution maturing in 2028 with income paid annually(*)

Hello %%Indv_Titl_Txt%% %%Indv_Last_Name%%,

 

At HSBC we offer you a choice of investment opportunities where you can diversify your savings and grow your money.

 

Which is why we’d like to introduce our newly launched HSBC Global Funds II ICAV – Euro Fixed Term Bond 2028. It’s a well-diversified portfolio, primarily investing in
Euro-denominated Investment Grade and Non-Investment Grade bonds issued by corporate issuers in developed markets.

 

The fund will be available for subscription until 30 April 2025.

Why choose to invest in this fund?

Aims to generate income paid annually*.
No initial and exit fees
Easy access - you can withdraw your money at any time without any exit fees, so your capital isn't tied
Offers a well-diversified portfolio in terms of sectors, countries and ratings backed by comprehensive research
The fund seeks to employ a buy and maintain, low rotation strategy

How can I apply?

  • You are an HSBC Bank Malta p.l.c. customer
  • You have €5,000 or more to invest
  • You are at least 18 years old
